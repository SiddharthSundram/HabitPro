Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is HabitPro's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We primarily collect:

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • Username (for your public profile)
  • Habit data (tracking logs, streaks, and notes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, operate, and maintain our website, to improve, personalize, and expand our website, and to analyze how you use our website.

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

4. Cookies

HabitPro uses "Cookies" to identify the areas of our website that you have visited. A Cookie is a small piece of data stored on your computer or mobile device by your web browser. We use Cookies to personalize the Content that you see on our website.
